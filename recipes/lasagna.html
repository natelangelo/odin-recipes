Lasagna

How to make lasagna:

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but it's worth the wait. Below comes a list of the ingredients you'll need and a step-by-step guide on how to make it.

Ingredients

    Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Lasagna noodles
  • cheeses
  • Egg

Step by Step

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
